🧠 Present Simple Exercises

1. Утвердительная форма

Вставь правильную форму глагола:

  • I ___ (work) every day.
  • She ___ (like) coffee.
  • They ___ (play) football on Sundays.
  • He ___ (watch) TV in the evening.
  • We ___ (live) in a big city.
  • She always ___ (go) to school early.
  • He often ___ (read) books.
  • They usually ___ (eat) at home.
  • I sometimes ___ (drink) tea.
  • She never ___ (be) late.

2. Отрицательная форма

Сделай отрицание:

  • I work every day → ______
  • She likes coffee → ______
  • They play football → ______
  • He watches TV → ______
  • We live here → ______
  • She goes to school → ______
  • He reads books → ______
  • They eat meat → ______
  • I drink milk → ______
  • She is happy → ______

3. Вопросы

Сделай вопрос:

  • You work here → ______
  • She likes coffee → ______
  • They play football → ______
  • He watches TV → ______
  • We live here → ______
  • She goes to school → ______
  • He reads books → ______
  • They eat meat → ______
  • You drink tea → ______
  • She is happy → ______

4. Смешанные упражнения

Выбери правильный вариант:

  • She (like / likes) coffee.
  • They (play / plays) football.
  • He (do / does) not work here.
  • (Do / Does) you like tea?
  • (Do / Does) she work here?
  • I (don’t / doesn’t) like milk.
  • He (don’t / doesn’t) read books.
  • We (live / lives) in a city.
  • She (watch / watches) TV.
  • They (is / are) happy.

5. Сложные упражнения (Intermediate)

Исправь ошибки или составь правильно:

  • She go to school every day.
  • I does not like coffee.
  • Do she play tennis?
  • He don’t work on Sundays.
  • They is happy.
  • She always drink coffee in the morning.
  • Does he likes pizza?
  • I never am late.
  • We goes to the gym twice a week.
  • He usually don’t watch TV.

🔥 Сложные упражнения (Intermediate+)

1. Переведи на английский

  • Он редко пьёт кофе по утрам.
  • Мы обычно ходим в спортзал по вечерам.
  • Она никогда не смотрит телевизор.
  • Ты часто опаздываешь?
  • Они не работают по выходным.

2. Раскрой скобки

  • She ___ (not / like) fast food.
  • They ___ (go) to school every day.
  • He ___ (usually / wake up) early.
  • We ___ (not / watch) TV in the morning.
  • ___ she ___ (play) tennis?

3. Исправь предложение

  • He do not likes coffee.
  • She don’t goes to school.
  • Do he works here?
  • They doesn’t play football.
  • I am go to work every day.

4. Составь предложение

  • always / she / late / is
  • play / they / often / football
  • never / I / coffee / drink
  • usually / he / early / wakes up
  • we / go / gym / to / the / twice / a week

5. Выбери правильный вариант

  • She (do / does) not like milk.
  • (Do / Does) they work here?
  • He (have / has) a car.
  • I (am / do) not understand.
  • They (is / are) usually busy.

6. Найди ошибку (лишнее слово)

  • She always is drinks coffee.
  • I do not likes tea.
  • He does plays football.
  • They are go to school every day.
  • We do goes to the gym.
