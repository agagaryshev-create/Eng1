🧠 Present Simple Exercises

1. Утвердительная форма

Вставь правильную форму глагола:

  • I ___ (work) every day.
  • She ___ (like) coffee.
  • They ___ (play) football on Sundays.
  • He ___ (watch) TV in the evening.
  • We ___ (live) in a big city.
  • She always ___ (go) to school early.
  • He often ___ (read) books.
  • They usually ___ (eat) at home.
  • I sometimes ___ (drink) tea.
  • She never ___ (be) late.

2. Отрицательная форма

Сделай отрицание:

  • I work every day → ______
  • She likes coffee → ______
  • They play football → ______
  • He watches TV → ______
  • We live here → ______
  • She goes to school → ______
  • He reads books → ______
  • They eat meat → ______
  • I drink milk → ______
  • She is happy → ______

3. Вопросы

Сделай вопрос:

  • You work here → ______
  • She likes coffee → ______
  • They play football → ______
  • He watches TV → ______
  • We live here → ______
  • She goes to school → ______
  • He reads books → ______
  • They eat meat → ______
  • You drink tea → ______
  • She is happy → ______

4. Смешанные упражнения

Выбери правильный вариант:

  • She (like / likes) coffee.
  • They (play / plays) football.
  • He (do / does) not work here.
  • (Do / Does) you like tea?
  • (Do / Does) she work here?
  • I (don’t / doesn’t) like milk.
  • He (don’t / doesn’t) read books.
  • We (live / lives) in a city.
  • She (watch / watches) TV.
  • They (is / are) happy.

5. Сложные упражнения (Intermediate)

Исправь ошибки или составь правильно:

  • She go to school every day.
  • I does not like coffee.
  • Do she play tennis?
  • He don’t work on Sundays.
  • They is happy.
  • She always drink coffee in the morning.
  • Does he likes pizza?
  • I never am late.
  • We goes to the gym twice a week.
  • He usually don’t watch TV.
