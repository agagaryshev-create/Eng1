🔥 Сложный уровень: 10 комплексных упражнений

Инструкция: в каждом упражнении вставь правильную форму (am / is / are / have / has) или сделай отрицание/вопрос.

1. To be (утвердительные предложения)

  • I ___ a student.
  • She ___ at home.
  • They ___ in the park.
  • We ___ ready.
  • It ___ cold today.

2. Have / Has (утвердительные)

  • I ___ a bike.
  • He ___ a new phone.
  • They ___ a big house.
  • She ___ two brothers.
  • We ___ an English lesson.

3. There is / There are

  • There ___ a book on the table.
  • There ___ two cats in the room.
  • There ___ a mistake in the text.
  • There ___ many students here.
  • There ___ a shop near my house.

4. To be (отрицание)

  • I am tired → I ___ tired.
  • She is happy → She ___ happy.
  • They are at school → They ___ at school.
  • We are late → We ___ late.
  • It is easy → It ___ easy.

5. Have / Has (отрицание)

  • I have a car → I ___ a car.
  • He has a dog → He ___ a dog.
  • She has a bike → She ___ a bike.
  • We have time → We ___ time.
  • They have money → They ___ money.

6. There is / There are (отрицание)

  • There is a problem → There ___ a problem.
  • There are apples → There ___ apples.
  • There is a cat → There ___ a cat.
  • There are students → There ___ students.
  • There is a mistake → There ___ a mistake.

7. To be (вопросы)

  • ___ I right?
  • ___ she your friend?
  • ___ they at home?
  • ___ we ready?
  • ___ it cold today?

8. Have / Has (вопросы)

  • ___ you a car?
  • ___ he a brother?
  • ___ she a phone?
  • ___ we a lesson today?
  • ___ they a house?

9. Mixed (choose correct form)

  • She ___ (be / have) a student.
  • They ___ (be / have) a big house.
  • He ___ (be / have) tired.
  • I ___ (be / have) a book.
  • We ___ (be / have) ready.

10. Mixed sentences (exam style)

  • There ___ (be) not any milk in the fridge.
  • She ___ (have) two sisters and a brother.
  • ___ (be) there a bank near here?
  • They ___ (be) very tired after school.
  • He ___ (have) a new idea.

🚀 Upper-Intermediate+ Grammar Practice (Advanced)

Инструкция: вставь правильную форму глагола (am / is / are / have / has) или сделай отрицание/вопрос. Обрати внимание на контекст.

1. Mixed (state vs possession)

  • She ___ very tired after work today.
  • He ___ a lot of experience in IT.
  • They ___ not interested in this project.
  • I ___ a meeting in 10 minutes.
  • We ___ ready to start the presentation.

2. There is / There are (uncountable + countable)

  • There ___ a lot of information in this report.
  • There ___ many people waiting outside.
  • There ___ some water in the bottle.
  • There ___ not enough chairs in the room.
  • ___ there any feedback from the client?

3. Have / Has (context + possession)

  • She ___ just finished her degree.
  • They ___ a lot of responsibilities at work.
  • He ___ not got enough time for this task.
  • I ___ never ___ such an experience before.
  • ___ you got any questions?

4. To be (natural speech context)

  • I ___ not sure about the decision.
  • It ___ getting late, we should go.
  • They ___ supposed to arrive at 6.
  • She ___ one of the best students in the group.
  • We ___ not allowed to use phones here.

5. Questions (natural English)

  • ___ she really interested in this job?
  • ___ they working on the new project?
  • ___ you got the report ready?
  • ___ there any problems with the system?
  • ___ he aware of the situation?

6. Negative forms (advanced structure)

  • She ___ not (be) involved in this case.
  • They ___ not (have) enough resources.
  • There ___ not (be) any clear explanation.
  • He ___ not (have) access to the system.
  • I ___ not (be) convinced by the argument.

7. Mixed grammar (choice required)

  • She ___ (is / has) been working here for 5 years.
  • There ___ (is / are) a number of issues to discuss.
  • He ___ (is / has) very confident in meetings.
  • They ___ (is / are) responsible for the report.
  • I ___ (am / have) never seen this before.

8. Real-life context sentences

  • There ___ a delay in the project delivery.
  • She ___ a strong background in finance.
  • They ___ not happy with the current results.
  • He ___ a meeting with the client right now.
  • ___ there a solution to this problem?

9. Error correction style

  • He are very busy today → ______
  • There is many people in the room → ______
  • She have a lot of experience → ______
  • They is not ready yet → ______
  • I has a question → ______

10. Exam-style mixed transformation

  • She is a manager → Make negative
  • They have a meeting → Make question
  • There are problems → Make negative
  • He has a car → Make question
  • I am ready → Make negative
