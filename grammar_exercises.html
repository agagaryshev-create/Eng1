🔥 Сложный уровень: 10 комплексных упражнений

Инструкция: в каждом упражнении вставь правильную форму (am / is / are / have / has) или сделай отрицание/вопрос.

1. To be (утвердительные предложения)

  • I ___ a student.
  • She ___ at home.
  • They ___ in the park.
  • We ___ ready.
  • It ___ cold today.

2. Have / Has (утвердительные)

  • I ___ a bike.
  • He ___ a new phone.
  • They ___ a big house.
  • She ___ two brothers.
  • We ___ an English lesson.

3. There is / There are

  • There ___ a book on the table.
  • There ___ two cats in the room.
  • There ___ a mistake in the text.
  • There ___ many students here.
  • There ___ a shop near my house.

4. To be (отрицание)

  • I am tired → I ___ tired.
  • She is happy → She ___ happy.
  • They are at school → They ___ at school.
  • We are late → We ___ late.
  • It is easy → It ___ easy.

5. Have / Has (отрицание)

  • I have a car → I ___ a car.
  • He has a dog → He ___ a dog.
  • She has a bike → She ___ a bike.
  • We have time → We ___ time.
  • They have money → They ___ money.

6. There is / There are (отрицание)

  • There is a problem → There ___ a problem.
  • There are apples → There ___ apples.
  • There is a cat → There ___ a cat.
  • There are students → There ___ students.
  • There is a mistake → There ___ a mistake.

7. To be (вопросы)

  • ___ I right?
  • ___ she your friend?
  • ___ they at home?
  • ___ we ready?
  • ___ it cold today?

8. Have / Has (вопросы)

  • ___ you a car?
  • ___ he a brother?
  • ___ she a phone?
  • ___ we a lesson today?
  • ___ they a house?

9. Mixed (choose correct form)

  • She ___ (be / have) a student.
  • They ___ (be / have) a big house.
  • He ___ (be / have) tired.
  • I ___ (be / have) a book.
  • We ___ (be / have) ready.

10. Mixed sentences (exam style)

  • There ___ (be) not any milk in the fridge.
  • She ___ (have) two sisters and a brother.
  • ___ (be) there a bank near here?
  • They ___ (be) very tired after school.
  • He ___ (have) a new idea.
