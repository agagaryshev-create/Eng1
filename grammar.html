📝 Упражнения

1. Глагол to be (am / is / are)

Пример: She ___ (be) happy → She is happy

  • I ___ (be) 14 years old.
  • He ___ (be) at school.
  • We ___ (be) friends.
  • They ___ (be) at home.

2. Have / Has

Пример: He ___ (have) a dog → He has a dog

  • I ___ (have) a brother.
  • She ___ (have) a bike.
  • They ___ (have) a lesson.
  • We ___ (have) homework.

3. There is / There are

Пример: There ___ (be) a book → There is a book

  • There ___ (be) a cat on the chair.
  • There ___ (be) two dogs in the yard.
  • There ___ (be) a shop near my house.
  • There ___ (be) many students in the class.

4. Смешанные задания

Пример: They ___ (have) a car → They have a car

  • She ___ (be) my sister.
  • We ___ (have) a big family.
  • There ___ (be) a park near the school.
  • He ___ (have) a new phone.
