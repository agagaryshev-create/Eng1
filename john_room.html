🏠 Description of John’s Room

His name is John. He is 15 years old and he is a student.

There is a small but cozy room in his house. There is a bed near the window and there is a desk next to it. There are some books and notebooks on the desk. There is a laptop that he uses for studying and playing games.

There are posters of football players on the wall. John has a big interest in football, so there are also a few footballs in the corner of the room.

There is a wardrobe with his clothes inside. There are not many clothes, but they are clean and tidy. There is a chair in front of the desk, and it is very comfortable.

John has a small shelf with video games and a few action figures. There is a lamp on the desk because he often studies in the evening.

There are headphones near the laptop. There is also a window with a nice view of the street.

The room is not very big, but it is very organized and comfortable. John is happy with his room because it is his personal space.
